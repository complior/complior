FAQ

Common questions

We just use ChatGPT — do we need this?

Yes. EU AI Act has specific obligations for deployers (companies that use AI tools). If your team uses ChatGPT, Copilot, or Midjourney — you have legal obligations under Articles 4, 26, and 27.

We're a US company. Why do we need EU compliance?

If you serve EU customers, hire EU employees, or your AI affects EU residents — the AI Act applies extraterritorially, same as GDPR.

How is this different from ISO/SOC2?

ISO 27001/SOC2 cover information security. EU AI Act covers AI-specific risks: bias, transparency, human oversight. They're complementary, not overlapping.

What if our tool isn't in the catalog?

Add any custom tool manually. Eva AI will interview you and classify risk in approximately 5 minutes.

Can we use generated docs for regulatory audits?

Yes. All generated documents follow EU AI Act legal requirements. Many customers submit Complior outputs directly to auditors.

How long does full setup take?

Most companies complete initial setup in 4–6 hours. Classification and training can start immediately after signup.
