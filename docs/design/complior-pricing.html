Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade instantly, downgrade at next billing cycle. No lock-in contracts on any plan.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

Your account converts to the Free plan automatically. No charge unless you explicitly upgrade. All your data is preserved.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs or startups?

Yes — 30% off Growth and Scale plans for registered NGOs, educational institutions, and startups under 2 years old. Contact us to apply.

Is my data stored in the EU?

100%. All data is hosted on Hetzner servers in Frankfurt, Germany. We use only EU-based subprocessors. Full GDPR compliance with DPA available on all plans.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), SEPA direct debit, and bank transfer for annual Enterprise plans. All payments processed via Stripe.

Can I pay annually?

Yes — save 20% with annual billing. Toggle the switch above to see annual prices. You can switch between monthly and annual at any time.
